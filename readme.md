### Stats App

notes: <br />
For decimal odds of 2.00 or greater, the formula is as follows: (decimal odds - 1) * 100 = American odds.<br />
For decimal odds between 1.01 and 1.99, the formula is as follows: -100 / (decimal odds - 1) = American odds.
